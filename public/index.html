FAQ
What is Rotator?
A free crypto rotation screener for the top Binance-listed coins. It helps you track your holdings, see which assets are outperforming, and identify potential rotation opportunities.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Everything is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. Your holdings are private and only visible to you.
How do I unlock Pro?
Click ⚡ Unlock Pro in the topbar, copy your unique referral link, and share it with friends. When 3 friends open your link and add at least one coin to their holdings, Pro unlocks automatically. It's completely free.
How often does data update?
Data loads when you open the app and when you click Refresh. Prices come from the CoinGecko API — free tier, so refresh rate is limited to avoid rate limits.
What does the score mean?
Each coin is ranked by 7D, 14D and 30D performance relative to the full list. The score is the average of those three rankings, scaled 0–100. Higher = stronger recent momentum.
Is this financial advice?
No. Rotator is an informational tool only. Always do your own research before making any investment decisions.
◎ How the Score Works
Each coin gets a score from 0 to 100 based on its recent price performance across three timeframes — 7 days, 14 days, and 30 days.
For each timeframe, every coin is ranked against all others (rank 1 = best performer). The three ranks are averaged, then converted to a 0–100 score. A coin that consistently outperforms across all timeframes scores highest.
65+
Strong momentum. This coin has been outperforming the field recently. Consider whether it's a rotation candidate — it may be overheated.
35–65
Average performance. Moving in line with the broader market. No strong signal either way.
0–35
Lagging. This coin has underperformed recently. It may be a rotation target — or there may be a fundamental reason. Always check the news.
The score is purely momentum-based. It does not account for fundamentals, news, or market cap. Use it as one signal among many — not as trading advice.
